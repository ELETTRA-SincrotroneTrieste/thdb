\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Instantiate a \doxyref{Historical\+View\+Widget}{p.}{classHistoricalViewWidget} and then register a specific widget associated to a specific Data\+Type. Then the switch\+Widget method will display the correct widget according to the Data\+Type in use.
